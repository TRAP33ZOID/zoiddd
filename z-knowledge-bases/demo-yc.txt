## Common Questions

**Q: Does it work with dogs?**
A: Technically yes, but dogs are way too straightforward. Every translation is basically "I LOVE YOU" or "FOOD PLEASE". Not enough data diversity. Also, our cats would be offended.

**Q: What's your most translated phrase?**
A: "The audacity of this human to be in MY house" - translates to approximately 40% of all meows.

**Q: Can it translate silent judgment?**
A: Working on it. Our ML model can detect 127 different types of cat stares. Current beta can identify "you're late with dinner" stare with 89% accuracy.

**Q: How did you collect training data?**
A: We recorded 10 million hours of cat vocalizations from 50,000 cats worldwide. Our CTO's cat alone contributed 200,000 hours (she's VERY opinionated).

**Q: What's your business model?**
A: SaaS (Sass-as-a-Service). Subscription revenue from desperate cat owners who just want to know if their cat actually loves them. (Spoiler: it's complicated.)
